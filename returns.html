Return and Cancellation Policy

At Claraglo Organic Skincare, we value our customers and strive to provide high-quality skincare products. If you are not completely satisfied with your purchase, we offer a return and cancellation policy as outlined below.

1. Eligibility for Returns

Products can be returned under the following conditions:

  • The return request is made within 14 days from the date of delivery.
  • The product is in its original packaging, unused, and in the same condition as received.
  • Items such as personal care products, opened skincare items, or used cosmetics are not eligible for returns due to hygiene reasons.
  • Proof of purchase (invoice or order confirmation email) is required.

2. How to Request a Return

To initiate a return, please follow these steps:

  • Email us at support@claraglo.com with your order number and reason for return.
  • Our customer support team will review your request and provide return shipping instructions.
  • Once we receive the returned product and verify its condition, a refund or exchange will be processed.

3. Refund Policy

Once your return is approved and received:

  • Refunds will be processed within 5-7 business days after product inspection.
  • The refund will be credited to your original payment method.
  • Shipping charges are non-refundable.

4. Order Cancellations

Orders can be canceled under the following conditions:

  • You request the cancellation within 24 hours of placing the order.
  • Once the order is shipped, cancellations will not be accepted.
  • To cancel your order, email us at support@claraglo.com with your order details.

5. Damaged or Incorrect Products

If you receive a damaged, defective, or incorrect product:

  • Contact us within 48 hours of delivery with images of the product.
  • We will arrange for a replacement or provide a full refund.
  • Return shipping for damaged or incorrect items will be covered by us.

6. Contact Us

If you have any questions regarding our return and cancellation policy, please reach out to our customer support team at support@claraglo.com.
